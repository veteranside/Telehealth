About Us

Welcome to TeleHealth Platform

We are focused on providing a mental healthcare Platform to reduce patient waiting time for consultant and minimize communication gap between doctor and patient. TeleHealth platform allows users to select the symptoms once they sign up and login. This is a very user-friendly and confidential platform. Our specialized doctors will evaluate the results and can ask more questions if necessary and provide the feedback, suggestions or pescriptions.
